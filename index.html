The Parachute Club is a non-profit organization founded in 2021 with the aim of creating the best jumping conditions and development opportunities for its members, as well as promoting the sport of parachuting. Below you will find the answer to the question of why a beginner parachuting enthusiast should choose our club?



Highly experienced instructors

The parachute club's ranks include more than a dozen instructors, all of whom have jumps between 500 and 2800 behind their backs.

Very good jumping plane

Built in 2016 and specially adapted for skydiving, the Pilatus PC-6 Porter, which takes ten paratroopers to a height of four kilometers in just 15 minutes.

Security and safety

The parachute club conducts only jumping trainings with square sports parachutes. Square sports parachutes are manageable and landing with them is soft when using the right landing technique. If done correctly, the probability of injury with square sports parachutes is significantly less than with round parachutes.

The airworthiness requirements and maintenance procedures for parachutes are set out in the Parachute Club's Parachuting Operations Manual approved by the Civil Aviation Administration. Each parachute set is subjected to a thorough inspection once a year, plus ongoing inspections if necessary.

The best development option for a paratrooper

The parachute club has de facto the widest knowledge of parachuting sports in Estonia. We fully support your future development as a paratrooper by organising in-service trainings (which are free of charge for club members!) and bringing world-class coaches to Estonia.

We don't do business

The parachute club is a non-profit organization in both form and content. Our goal is not to make a profit, but to create better jumping conditions and opportunities for club members. We do not have an owner and we direct all the money earned to the maintenance and development of the club's aircraft and infrastructure. The club team does things out of joy and love for the sport of parachuting, not for money.
