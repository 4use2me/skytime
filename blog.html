In the first week of July, you can jump with a parachute in Kihnu

On 1-6 July, the Estonian Parachute Club's training camp will take place on the island of Kihnu, during which kihnans and visitors to the island will also have the opportunity to try out the tandem jump with an experienced parachute instructor. The Estonian Parachute Club, which usually operates at Rapla airfield, will move to Kihnu on the evening of Friday, June 30. The first paratroopers will land at Kihnu airfield around 19:00 on Friday, in case of suitable weather, jumping out of the plane that took off from the mainland.
